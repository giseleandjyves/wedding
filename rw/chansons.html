Mana idukunda byahebuje

Instrumentales
  • ⁀♪‿⁀♪‿
  • Refrain :
    Mana idukunda byahebuje shimirwa ibitangaza udukorera. x2(uniquement pour le début)
    Aya majwi yacu nakunyure mubyeyi uturinze,
    tugutaramiye tugutetera mubyeyi utaduhana.
  • ⁀♪‿⁀♪‿
  • Uko ibitero biririmbwa :
  • 1. Watwihangiye udukunze mugenga , uturema mu ishusho yawe , turabigushimiye
  • 2. Waduhaye umugisha n'umwuka wawe , uduha kugenga ibyo waremye byose , turabigushimiye
  • 3. Waducunguje umwana wawe w'ikinege , aturokora ingoyi y'umwanzi gica , turabigushimiye
  • 4. Waduhaye ishema kiliziya ukunda , ubumwe bw'abakwemera bakugana , turabigushimiye .
  • 5. N'iyo tugucumuyeho ntiducibwa , kuko ukunda nyir'umutima wicuza , turabigushimiye .
  • 6. Mu bihe by'amage ni wowe buhungiro , uwaguhungiyeho ntahungabana , turabugushimiye.
  • 7. Ntawakwiringiye wikoreye amaboko , kuko ibyaremwe byose bigengwa nawe , turabigushimiye .
  • ⁀♪‿⁀♪‿

Traduction :

Most loving lord, we thank you for the many miracles you did for us. May our voices please you, oh protecting father, We enjoy your care in praise, as you never abandon us.

1. You created us with love, almighty King, in your own image, we thank you lord.
2. You blessed us with your Holy Spirit, you endowed us with ower over your creatures, we thank you lord.
3. You redeemed us with your only Son, who delivered us from Satan's hold, we thank you lord.
4. You gave us pride in your beloved Church, The unity of the believers proceed you, we thank you lord.
5. Even when we sin we are not cursed, because you love the guilty who seeks forgiveness, we thank you lord.
6. In dire moments you are the safe haven, no one is shaken under your protection, we thank you lord.
7. No one trusting you feels hopeless, because you govern all your creatures, we thank you lord.
